# Pilot

A pilot selects their equipment from the platform list, flies missions, and uploads drone imagery.

## Key commands

- [[inflights gear list]] — browse available equipment on the platform
- [[inflights gear add]] — add equipment to your profile
- [[inflights gear mine]] — list equipment on your profile
- [[inflights gear remove]] — remove equipment from your profile
- [[inflights gear rfc]] — request new equipment to be added to the platform
- [[inflights proposal list]] — see flight proposals assigned to you
- [[inflights proposal show]] — view proposal details
- [[inflights proposal accept]] — accept a proposal (becomes a flight)
- [[inflights proposal reject]] — reject a proposal with a reason
- [[inflights flights]] — see assigned flights
- [[inflights flight show]] — check flight details and area
- [[inflights flight add]] — register a flight done outside the platform
- [[inflights upload images]] — upload drone images after a flight
